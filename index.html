Why elbow protection sells

A physical barrier for the front legs — the use cases your customers are already searching for. (Protective garments, not medical devices.)

🛡️ Elbow & skin protection

Large, heavy dogs rest their elbows on hard floors all day. A padded sleeve puts a soft, breathable layer between elbow and ground — exactly where coats wear thin and skin gets rough.

🚫 Lick & chew barrier

A comfortable alternative to the plastic cone for dogs that won't leave a front-leg spot alone — soft coverage the dog forgets about, instead of a collar it fights.

🧼 Keeps wounds-adjacent areas clean

After grooming, minor scrapes or vet visits, owners want the front leg covered and clean on walks. Breathable cotton with a cooling-silk contact layer works in warm weather too.

📈 An under-served niche

Most overseas front-leg protection tops out at medium sizes and clinical looks. Original prints + true large-breed sizing is a listing that stands out instead of competing on price.

How to measure

  1. A · Sleeve Length — from the top of the shoulder down along the front leg to where you want the cuff to sit.
  2. B · Limb Circumference — around the thickest part of the front leg (usually just below the elbow). This is the primary fit measurement.
  3. C · Cuff Opening — around the leg where the cuff will sit; the ribbed cuff stretches, so match or slightly exceed this.

Fit by B + A first; body weight is a secondary reference. When in doubt between two sizes for a fluffy or muscular dog, we'll advise — send us breed + weight + these two measurements.

Ship via your freight forwarder

You arrange your own freight forwarder and send us the forwarder's warehouse address in China. We deliver the goods domestically with a packing list; your forwarder handles the international leg and customs.

Why bring your own forwarder? B2B buyers usually already have a preferred shipping channel and rates. Keeping the international leg on your side means no markup from us, full visibility for you, and the shipping method (air / sea / dedicated line) that fits your schedule. We support you with accurate weights, packing data and the documents your forwarder needs.
