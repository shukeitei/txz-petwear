How ordering works

A simple wholesale flow — you keep control of your own international shipping.

Send an inquiry

Email us the designs, sizes and quantities you're interested in (mixed designs/sizes are fine). Tell us your market and whether you need dropship packaging.

Get a quotation

We reply with a wholesale price list, production lead time, and current stock/supply status for each design.

Confirm & pay

Confirm the order and pay by bank transfer (T/T). Payment details come with the quotation.

Production & QC

Typical lead time is around 2–3 weeks depending on quantity — we run a partner factory line and an in-house studio line in parallel, and can part-ship sizes that finish early.

Ship via your freight forwarder

You arrange your own freight forwarder and send us the forwarder's warehouse address in China. We deliver the goods domestically with a packing list; your forwarder handles the international leg and customs.

Why bring your own forwarder? B2B buyers usually already have a preferred shipping channel and rates. Keeping the international leg on your side means no markup from us, full visibility for you, and the shipping method (air / sea / dedicated line) that fits your schedule. We support you with accurate weights, packing data and the documents your forwarder needs.

FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity?

We're small-batch friendly. Trial orders with mixed designs and sizes are welcome — tell us what you have in mind and we'll confirm feasibility and pricing by quantity tier.

Can you do private label / OEM labeling?

Yes. We can sew in your labels — label content and trademark authorization are provided by you as the brand owner. As the importer of record, regulatory responsibility for your market stays on the buyer side; we support with signed fiber-content declarations, "Made in China" origin, and care instructions.

Do you provide product photos we can use?

Yes — white-background photos, detail shots and model-worn shots for every design, watermark-free and free of Chinese text, ready for marketplace listings.

Can you support dropshipping?

Yes. Each pair is packed in its own clear zip poly bag with per-pair weights documented, so per-order fulfillment through your forwarder or warehouse is straightforward.

Will a design I list stay available?

We tell you upfront which designs are stable for restock and which are being phased out, so you never build a listing on a style we can't keep supplying.

Which markets do you currently serve?

We supply resellers in North America and are open to buyers in Europe and other regions. Products are designed for medium and large breeds.
